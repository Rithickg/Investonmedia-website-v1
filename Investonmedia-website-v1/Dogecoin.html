What is Dogecoin ?


Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency payment system made as a joke to make fun of the speculation around the cryptocurrencies at that time. The logo of the Dogecoin became a meme which made it popular around the crypto market.


Who invented Dogecoin ?

Dogecoin was created by software engineers Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer. It was interduced in December 6, 2013.


Why Dogecoin is popular around the crypto market ?

Dogecoin became popular as it is already made for meme. It gained more popular when Elon Musk started to mention and talk about Dogecoin in Twitter. Elon Musk even said that he has Dogecoin and Bitcoin in a interview. The community around the Dogecoin helped it to increase its popularity in recent years.


What is the future of Dogecoin ?

When it comes to the future of the Dogecoin it seems very impressive as the Ethereum founder Vitalik Buterin and an adviser to Elon Musk joined the Dogecoin board for its development in the future.
