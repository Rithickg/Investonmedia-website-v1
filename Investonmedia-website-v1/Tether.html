What is Tether ?


Tether is a cryptocurrency issued by Tether Limited. Tether was originally named as "Realcoin" which was announced on 6th October 2014 built on the Bitcoin Blockchain. Later on 20th November 2014 ,"Realcoin" was renamed as "Tether". The token was issued and controlled by Bitfinex, a Hong Kong based cryptocurrency exchange owned and operated by iFinex inc.


Why Tether is always stable and stays on 1$ ?


Tether is known as "StableCoin", these digital currencies that are tied to real world assets like US-Dollar. Tether is token version of US dollar which is always maintained at 1:1 ration with US dollar. The Tether is fully reserved with the sum of all the Tether tokens in circulation is less than or equal to value of our reserves.


Why Tether is safe to invest in Crypto Market ?


Unlike other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin ,Ethereum which are always on high fluctuation that can be a huge threat to the investors. Whereas Tether always maintain its value with the US Dollar which is stable and reduce the risk of price drop.


Why Tether is controversial ?


Tether faced various controversy in the recent years which caused a drawback to its reputation in the crypto market. In 2018 ,Tether failed to prove that the Token was backed by actual currencies in the bank. The company was also reported for its artificially inflated Bull Run in 2017 leading Bitcoin to reach record high of around $20,000.
