What is Ethereum ?


Ethereum is a popular decentralized ,open source blockchain with smart contract functionality.It has the second largest market capitalization in the cryptocurrency after Bitcoin. It has its own programming language called Solidity.


Who invented Ethereum ?

Ethereum was invented by programmer Vitalik Buterin in 2013 and on 30 july 2015 ,it went live. Popular uses of Ethereum are creation of fungible(ERC20) and non-fungible (ERC721) tokens,decentralized finance, decentralized exchange, games and gambling.


Why Ethereum is popular ?

Ethereum is a technology that lets you send cryptocurrency to anyone for a small fee. It also powers applications that everyone can use and no one can take down. It's the world's programmable blockchain. Ethereum builds on Bitcoin's innovation, with some big differences. Both let you use digital money without payment providers or banks.

What is Ethereum 2.0 ?

Ethereum 2.0 also known as Serenity or ETH 2.0 is an upgrade to Ethereum. It's objective is to increase the Ethereum's capacity ,reduce fees and make more sustainable. So it will change its mechanism from proof-of-work(PoW) to proof-of-stake(PoS).


Proof-of-stake allows to make faster transactions and lower fees compared to its previous method proof-of-work.
