What is Binance Coin ?


Binance coin is a cryptocurrency issued by Binance exchange with BNB symbol.It was launched in July 2017 with Initial Coin Offering (ICF). Binance coin is initially based on ethereum network ERC-20 and now it is shifted to its own Blockchain -The Binance Chain.


who made Binance ?

Binance was founded by Changpeng Zhao, a developer who created high frequency trading software. Binance was a China based company which later moved out of china due to regulation of cryptocurrency in China.


Why Binance coin is popular?

Binance is a cryptocurrency exchange platform for trading cryptocurrencies. Binance is the largest exchange platform in the world for trading. Already being the best trading platform helped the Binance coin to reach popularity among the traders.


Is Binance Banned ?

Binance was banned in United States on regulatory grounds in 2019. Binance then made a saperate Binance.US exchange registered with United States Financial Crimes Enforcement Network with applicable US laws.


Binance was also ordered to stop all regulated activity in United Kingdom in june 2021. Binance is currently under investigation on allegation of money laundering and tax offenses by United States Department of Justice and Internal Revenue Service.


What is Binance Burn ?

Binance use 20% of its profit to buy back and burn Binance coins to destroy them completely. Binance continues to burn every quaterly and destroys over 100 million coins , over 50% of total supply. But the question is why do they burn ? The answer is it ensures the finite supply of Binance coin in circulation which helps it to be more valuable and scarce.
