What is Bitcoin ?


Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency without a intermediate like bank or an administration that can send user to user transactions without the need for intermediate. The transactions are verified by network nodes and recorded in a public distributed ledger called as Blockchain.


Who invented Bitcoin ?

It was invented by an unknown person or a group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008. The currency began use in 2009 when it was released as open source.


Why Bitcoin is popular ?

As being the first cryptocurrency it gained huge popularity in the future years after its launch. Though being popular bitcoin has also been criticized for its use in illegal transactions and illegal activities. Bitcoin can also be exchanged for other currencies ,products and can be used in real-world.


How does Bitcoin work ?

Each Bitcoin is basically a computer file stored in a digital wallet on a computer or smartphone. People can send and receive Bitcoin to others by their digital wallet. Every transaction is recorded in a ledger called Blockchain
